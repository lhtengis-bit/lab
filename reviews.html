About Wooting

Meaningful innovation, driven by community

Founded in 2015 and based in the Netherlands, Wooting builds keyboards guided by feedback from its community. The company pioneered analog Hall-effect gaming keyboards and the Rapid Trigger feature that's now an industry standard.

  • Community-driven development on Discord and Reddit
  • Four-year warranty and lifetime support
  • Backward compatibility — new features come to older boards too
  • Open Analog and RGB SDKs for developers
